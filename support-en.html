Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with SmartMem?

After downloading and installing the SmartMem app, you can start using it immediately. No account registration required, all data is stored on your device.

How do I add learning content?

Tap the '+' button on the main interface and enter the content you want to memorize. You can add various forms of learning materials including text and images.

What is smart review reminder?

SmartMem uses scientific memory algorithms to intelligently calculate optimal review times, reminding you to review at the right moment, improving memory efficiency.

What features are included in the annual subscription?

Annual subscription (¥128/year) includes: unlimited learning content, advanced memory algorithms, detailed learning statistics, multiple theme choices, desktop widgets, and other premium features.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage your SmartMem subscription in iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions and cancel anytime. You'll continue to have access to premium features until the current billing period ends.

Will my data be lost?

Your learning data is safely stored on your device and automatically backed up through iCloud. Even when switching devices, your data will be automatically restored.

Which devices are supported?

SmartMem supports iPhone and iPad, requiring iOS 14.0 or later. Desktop widget functionality is also supported.

How do I use desktop widgets?

Long press on your iPhone home screen, tap the '+' in the top left corner, search for SmartMem, and select the appropriate widget size to add to your home screen.
