Privacy Policy

Introduction

SmartMem ("we", "our", or "this app") values your privacy. This privacy policy explains our data processing practices. SmartMem is a completely local app, and we do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information.

We Do Not Collect Any Information

SmartMem is designed as a completely local application, which means:

  • We do not collect any personally identifiable information
  • The app does not require account registration or personal information
  • We do not collect usage statistics or analytics data
  • We do not track your usage behavior
  • No ads in the app, no third-party tracking

Local Data Storage

All your learning data is securely stored on your device:

  • All learning content and progress is stored locally on your device only
  • Automatically backed up through iOS system's iCloud backup feature (managed by Apple)
  • We have no servers storing any of your data
  • You have complete control over your data

No Information Sharing

Since we do not collect any information, therefore:

  • We do not share data with any third parties
  • We do not sell any user information
  • We do not use data for marketing purposes
  • Subscription information is only processed and managed by Apple

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, your information security depends on your device's security settings. We recommend keeping your device updated and using appropriate security measures.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal information, traditional data rights (access, correction, deletion) are not applicable. However, you have complete control over all data stored locally on your device.

Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through in-app notifications. Continued use of our service indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
